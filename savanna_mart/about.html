About Savanna Mart

Savanna Mart is a student prototype website modelled around African e-commerce principles: marketplace selling, delivery support, digital payments, product discovery and social community engagement.

The website uses HTML5 semantic structure, external CSS, responsive navigation, keyboard focus, skip links, alternative text and a page search tool.
